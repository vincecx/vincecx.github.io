Chen Xu is a PhD candidate in Mechanical Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology \(HKUST\), under the supervision of [Prof. Shuhuai Yao](https://rraynachen.wixsite.com/website). He holds MEng and BEng degrees from Dalian University of Technology, where he was previously supervised by [Prof. Xuehu Ma](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=SpqNo8MAAAAJ&hl=en) during his master’s studies. 

Chen’s research focuses on *fluid dynamics*, *phase change*, and *interfacial science*. His research mainly includes:

- Vaporization-induced fluid dynamics under extreme conditions
- Spreading and wicking on superhydrophilic surfaces
- Thin-film evaporation
- Leidenfrost effect
